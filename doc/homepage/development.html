If you'd like to contact us to report a bug or submit a patch, please use the tracker on RubyForge.

Please see the contact page about contacting developers or mailing lists.

The git repository is at git://hollensbe.org/git/ruby-dbi. The SVN and CVS trees of old are deprecated, and development has been applied far beyond what they cover. Please do not use them!

Ruby/DBI is a BSD-licensed product. As a result, the licensing on your patches must reflect the same. Please do not send patches that are of any other license. Thanks!
